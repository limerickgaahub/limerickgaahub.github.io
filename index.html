About

Limerick GAA Hub is designed to provide a clear and intuitive overview of fixtures and results — easily filterable by competition, team, or date.

Over time, the aim is to build an archive of historical results.

Please note: this site is not associated with Limerick GAA — it is run solely by a club member and Limerick supporter sharing resources.
